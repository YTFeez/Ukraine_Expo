The beginning of the war

The war started in February 2022 when Russia invaded Ukraine. Cities were destroyed and millions fled their homes. Kyiv was attacked but Ukrainian forces defended it bravely.

Humanitarian Crisis

Over 8 million Ukrainians became refugees, fleeing to Poland, Romania, and Germany. Families were separated and many lost everything. The world showed solidarity through aid and support.

Crimea Day ‚Äì Dress Code

On Crimea Day, we wear the colors of the Ukrainian flag to show our support and remember the people affected by the war. Each color has a special meaning.

Blue

Represents the sky and peace. It reminds us of freedom and hope for a better future.

Yellow

Symbolizes wheat fields and prosperity. It represents the strength and resilience of Ukraine.

White

Stands for peace and unity. It's our message that war must end and harmony must return.
