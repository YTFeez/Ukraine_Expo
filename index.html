The beginning of the war

The war started in February 2022 when Russia invaded Ukraine. Cities were destroyed and millions fled their homes. Kyiv was attacked but Ukrainian forces defended it bravely.

deportation of the Ukrainian population

Over 8 million Ukrainians became refugees, moved to Poland, Romania, Germany, France... Families were separated and many lost everything. The world showed solidarity through aid and support. In this picture, the little girl has lost her family.
